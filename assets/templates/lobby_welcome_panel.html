How to Play

Pretend You're Xyzzy is a Cards Against Humanity clone. Each round, one player is the Card Czar and reads a black prompt card. Everyone else plays white answer cards face-down. The Czar picks the funniest answer — that player wins the round. First to 5 points wins the game.

  • Pick a room on the left, or create your own.
  • The room host selects card packs and starts the game.
  • Change your nickname at any time using the section below.
